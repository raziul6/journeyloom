FAQ

Questions, answered

Is the free version really fully functional?
Yes. Everything in the free plugin is complete and unrestricted — trips, hotels, the booking engine, search, reviews, wishlist & compare, emails, reports and bank-transfer checkout. There is no locked code and no trial limits.
How does the Pro upgrade work?
Pro is a separate add-on plugin (hosted at byteflows.net). Install and activate it alongside the free plugin — the extra options appear automatically in your existing settings. Your trips, hotels, bookings and settings are untouched; there's nothing to migrate.
What if Pro isn't right for me?
Every Pro plan comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If it's not the right fit, email us within 14 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked. And the free plugin keeps working either way.
Which payment gateways are supported?
Manual / bank transfer works out of the box in the free plugin. The Pro add-on adds Stripe (cards with SCA / 3-D Secure), PayPal and Razorpay — all with server-side verification. Developers can add more gateways via the wptm_payment_gateways filter.
Do I need an AI subscription for the AI features?
No subscription from us — the AI assistant is bring-your-own-key. Connect your OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), or any OpenAI-compatible API key (Groq, Ollama, OpenRouter…) and pay your provider directly. Nothing is sent to any AI provider until you install Pro, enable AI and enter your key.
Will it work with my theme and page builder?
Yes. The plugin ships native Gutenberg blocks and Elementor widgets, plus shortcodes that work in any builder. Front-end styling uses CSS custom properties so it adapts to your brand, and every template can be overridden in your theme.
Can I try it with sample data?
Absolutely — the built-in demo importer adds 12 sample trips and 12 hotels (with images, pricing, itineraries and rooms) in one click, and can remove them just as easily.
